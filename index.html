TakaraTone

Build playlists & discover music — on your own computer

A free, local desktop app that curates playlists onto Tidal, Spotify, and YouTube Music, with chart time-travel, live concerts, and artist news. No account with us; nothing uploaded.

⬇ Download for Windows Read the manual

What it does

Make playlists

From a vibe, artists, songs, a genre & decade, a soundtrack, or “surprise me” — then send them to your service.

Wayback Machine

Recreate a year’s top chart songs — Hot 100, Rock, Country, or R&B.

This Day in Jams

Free live concert recordings from this date in history, with an in-app player and cast to your speakers.

Artist News & Shows

Follow bands for recent news and upcoming concerts near you, with ticket prices.

First launch: because the app isn’t code-signed, Windows may show a blue “Windows protected your PC” notice. Click More info → Run anyway — it appears only once. Full setup details are in the user manual.

Get started

Download and run the installer above (or grab the latest from the Releases page), then connect a music service in Settings. Most features are free with no key; adding a free Google Gemini key unlocks the AI playlist modes. The manual walks through every screen and every key.
